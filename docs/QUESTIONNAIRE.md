## Change history

This is the second revision of the V4 question set (Jul 20 update), shortened
from 32 to 26 questions across the same 15→16 module count. Changes from the
previous revision:

**Cut entirely** (no longer asked): bathroom exhaust fan status, drinking
water source, water-related concerns, detailed flooring type, sustainability
actions already in place, maintenance checklist (smoke/CO alarms, dryer vent,
gutters, under-sink leaks), maintenance confidence rating.

**Added:** a new "Sound" question about home noise levels.

**Restructured:** "Your home" split into "Your home" (type, age, ZIP) +
"Your plans" (ownership, plans); "Your capacity" split into "Your capacity"
(budget) + "Time and DIY comfort" (DIY comfort, weekly time); "Air and
moisture" + "Air and systems" merged into "Moisture and air"; "Light and
comfort" + part of "Comfort and function" merged into "Comfort"; "Clutter"
and "Recent changes" became their own standalone sections.

**Fewer required questions:** down from 11 to 7 — home type, home age,
ownership, plans, DIY comfort, and weekly time are now all optional.
