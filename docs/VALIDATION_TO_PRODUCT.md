# Validation Sequence: Questionnaire to Finished Product

## Gate 0 — Problem interviews
**Build:** clickable prototype or current V4 form; no payment code.
**Recruit:** 15–20 people in the chosen beachhead segment.
**Learn:** what triggered concern, what they tried, where generic AI failed, and what decision they postponed.
**Pass when:** at least 10 describe the same high-friction job and 6 ask for the result or next step without prompting.

## Gate 1 — Snapshot usefulness
**Build:** responsive form + manually reviewed or rule-based three-action Snapshot.
**Volume:** 30–50 completed Snapshots.
**Measure:** completion, time, usefulness rating, action selected, qualitative confusion.
**Pass when:** ≥60% rate 4/5 or 5/5; ≥40% select an action they intend to do; ≥50% reach results after starting.
**Change, do not add features, if these fail.**

## Gate 2 — Willingness to pay
**Build:** paid 90-day Home Roadmap delivered through the app/PDF; concierge behind the scenes is acceptable.
**Test:** $29, $49, and $79 positioning with the same core deliverable across cohorts.
**Pass when:** 10+ strangers pay; landing-to-purchase ≥3% from qualified traffic; refund rate <10%; buyers can explain the value in their own words.

## Gate 3 — Repeated value
**Build:** saved Home Profile, project checklist, seasonal follow-up, one useful monthly refresh.
**Do not call it a subscription until behavior supports it.**
**Pass when:** 30–50 paying users; ≥35% return in month two; ≥25% complete or update a project; at least 10 say they would be disappointed to lose the product.

## Gate 4 — Subscription
**Build:** “This month / This season / Later,” maintenance reminders, progress, AI chat grounded only in the Home Profile.
**Price test:** $12–$24/month or $99–$199/year.
**Pass when:** 100 paying subscribers, month-three retention ≥65%, monthly churn trending below 6–8%, support remains manageable.

## Gate 5 — Photo-assisted profile
**Build only after recurring value is proven.** Photos should suggest visible categories and ask users to confirm them. Never claim hidden conditions from images.
**Pass when:** photo flow reduces onboarding time or unanswered questions by ≥25% and does not materially increase corrections or support.

## Gate 6 — Professional and partner product
Architect/designer dashboard, co-branded reports, builder/remodeler lead tools, employer or insurer wellness education pilots. Validate each channel separately.

## Finished-product definition
A finished first product is not every imagined feature. It is a reliable web product with acquisition, onboarding, saved Home Profile, prioritized roadmap, project completion loop, billing, retention, support, privacy controls, analytics, and evidence that customers repeatedly receive value.

## Million-dollar paths
- 5,000 members × $17/month ≈ $1.02M ARR.
- 2,500 members × $20/month + 1,000 annual roadmaps × $199 ≈ $799k; add 200 Pro seats × $1,200/year ≈ $1.04M.
These are business-model scenarios, not forecasts.
